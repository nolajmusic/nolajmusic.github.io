About Nolaj

With his first mixtape Nolaj is Power (2014), the Detroit, Michigan MC made his statement of intent in the rap game. Hits like "Ozwald Boateng" , the smooth storytelling track "Uncle Jody" and the soulful "Lorine Chia" Nolaj displays the incredible range and versatility that have people hanging on his every word.

Nolaj's secong mixtape scheduled for release in Spring 2015, has the underground rap scene buzzing. He promises it will be his most refined work yet. "I think I’m somebody that’s finally finding my groove as an artist. My message is unique and I have been blessed with the talent to deliver it through my music" he says.

Jalon Perryman was known more for putting people on posters as a basketball star than dropping bars. What was once a hobby while on a basketball scholarship at Ball State University became a calling. Jalon took on the name Nolaj (Jalon spelled backwards) and began his journey start rapping back in his hometown of Detroit.
